Leverage

The cockpit does two things.

01

Identify Leverage

Synthesizes everything across your tools and surfaces what actually moves the needle. No more digging through Slack, email, and dashboards.

02

Help You Act

Drafts the email. Builds the report. Runs the research. Doesn't just inform — executes on your behalf.

The result: you do more, with less effort, in less time.
